Breaded Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp salted butter
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 4 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 2 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 large handful of fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Rub chicken with 1 tbsp olive oil and lemon zest, season with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour.
  2. Heat remaining 2 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken until golden, about 5 minutes per side. Remove chicken.
  3. In the same skillet, sear lemon slices until caramelized. Remove from pan.
  4. Add tomatoes, garlic, thyme, and red pepper flakes. Cook until tomatoes burst.
  5. Stir in wine and lemon juice, scraping up browned bits. Return chicken to skillet and simmer until cooked through.
  6. Top with basil and caramelized lemon slices.

Buffalo Ranch Chicken and Cheese Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup buffalo wing sauce
  • 1/4 cup ranch salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
  • Tortilla chips for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix chicken, cream cheese, buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, and sour cream. Spread in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve with tortilla chips.

15-Minute Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Add to skillet and brown on one side.
  3. Whisk together honey, soy sauce, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Pour sauce over chicken and cook until chicken is cooked through.
